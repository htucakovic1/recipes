Fluffy Pancakes

The goldilocks of pancakes, these promise to turn out just right. “The best pancakes we’ve found," says Allstar Bri Evans. "We make these on Sundays when the family is home together and wants to spend time smiling and cutting up at the table. The kids love to help me make them.”

Pancakes

  • cup of milk
  • 2 tablespoons white distilled vinegar
  • 1 cup all-purpose flour
  • 2 tablespoons white sugar
  • 1 teaspoon baking powder

Cinnamon

  • 5 tablespoons packed brown sugar
  • one quarter cup of butter, melted
  • one and half teaspoons of ground cinnamon

Cream cheese icing

  • one quarter cup butter,softened
  • 2 ounces cream cheese, at room temperature
  • cup of confectioners' sugar
